How it works

Run the Bridge

Download the free Mac app and double-click. It starts your agent host automatically — no terminal, no config.

Scan the QR

Open Pounce on your phone, tap Sync, and point the camera at the code in the Bridge window. That's the whole setup.

Pounce from anywhere

Your sessions sync instantly. Steer agents, reply, review diffs, and ship — one-handed, from the couch or the commute.
