How Pounce connects to your computer

Pounce doesn't run your agents in the cloud — it's a remote control for the coding agents already running on your own machine. You install a tiny free Bridge app on your computer, scan one QR code with your phone, and you're paired.

No account. No Pounce server in the middle. Your code and conversations stay on your devices.

Good to know

Common questions

Don't have the phone app yet?

iOS & Android are in private beta — public launch is coming soon. Grab the free Mac Bridge now so you're ready on day one.

Phone and computer on different networks?

Expose the Bridge through a tunnel — Tailscale, ngrok, or a Cloudflare tunnel — and scan the same QR. Pounce connects to whatever address the Bridge advertises.

Is the connection secure?

Each machine pairs with its own private token, and the production path uses a direct peer-to-peer identity to reach home. There's no shared cloud inbox for your sessions.

What can it actually do?

Stream every agent live, reply or redirect by voice, approve permission prompts, and review diffs — then commit, push, or open a PR, all from the phone.

Ready to set it up?

Install the free, notarized Mac Bridge, then scan once. You'll be steering your agents from your pocket in about two minutes.
